Domestic Travel

I have currently visited the following states; CA, OR, AZ, NV, NY, LA, IL, NY, KY, TN, TX, CO, FL, NC, GA, MO. Listed below are five of my favorite places I have visited

Columbia River Gorge, OR

I spent four days hiking multiple trails in the Columbia River Gorge. It was one of the most majestic places I have ever seen

Yosemite National Park, CA

I spent only one day here when I was fourteen, but layed the foundation for my desire to travel.

New Orleans, LA

I have visited NOLA multiple times, and each time it steals a little more of my soul. The grittiness and pulse of the city are so seductive.

Atlanta, GA

The ATL, another city I have made repeat visits. My brother from another mother lives in Atlanta, and is the main reason I keep going back.

Sedona, AZ

Very few places emit an energy like Sedona does. It's difficult to describe. There is a force that naturally energizes your soul. The Red rock, the lush greenry, and endless sky create an amaing sight.
